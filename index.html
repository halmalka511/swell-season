Explore

Things to DoAround the Lake

Lake Chelan is a world unto itself — 50.5 miles of pristine glacial water, over 300 sunny days a year, and adventures for every kind of traveler.

⛵

Boating & Water Sports

Rent a boat or jet ski right at Wapato Point or from Mill Bay Marine in Manson. Kayaks, canoes, paddleboards, and pedal boats are also available at the resort Kiosk on the beach. Lake Chelan is over 50 miles of pristine blue water perfect for wakeboarding, water skiing, and scenic cruising.

🚢

Lady of the Lake — Stehekin

Take the famous ferry to Stehekin, a remote community of ~75 people accessible only by boat, float plane, or a long hike. The ride itself is stunning. Grab a pastry at the Stehekin Pastry Company — one of the most talked-about bakeries in the Northwest.

💦

Slidewaters Waterpark

Located in downtown Chelan, Slidewaters is a beloved family waterpark with thrilling slides and pools — perfect for a hot summer afternoon. Open seasonally; check their website for current hours.

🥾

Hiking & State Parks

Lake Chelan State Park is just 2 miles away. Twenty-Five Mile Creek State Park (9 miles) offers more remote hiking and camping. Both feature beautiful lake views and trails suitable for all fitness levels.

🎪

Manson Farmers Market

Wednesday and Saturday mornings 8:30 AM – Noon (June–September) at the Manson Grange Hall on Wapato Way. Fresh local produce, baked goods, flowers, and artisan goods from the valley's talented farmers and makers.

🎿

Winter Fun (Seasonal)

Echo Ridge Nordic Ski Area offers cross-country skiing, snowshoeing, sledding and tubing just up the road. Over 170 miles of groomed snowmobile trails make this area equally magical in the winter months.

🎰

12 Tribes Lake Chelan Casino

The local casino offers slots, table games, dining, and live entertainment — a fun evening option rain or shine.

Dining

Where toEat & Drink

From blueberry farm breakfasts to lake-view fine dining, the Manson–Chelan corridor has something for every appetite. Downtown Manson is less than a mile away.

In & Around Manson

Blueberry Hill Farm

Breakfast & Lunch · Farm Café

A local gem and a must-visit. Situated on a working blueberry farm, the café serves legendary blueberry cinnamon rolls, hearty egg scrambles, and fresh baked goods. U-pick blueberries in summer. Arrive early — it fills up fast!

Lake Chelan Brewery

Craft Beer & Food · Manson

Downtown Manson's beloved gathering spot. Craft beers made with local ingredients, plus a diverse food menu — known for their Philly cheesesteak, Reuben, and onion rings. Grab 4-packs to go for the lake.

Mi Paloma

Mexican · Manson

Family recipes served in a cheerful downtown Manson spot. Famous for their Baja fried fish tacos, mango margaritas, and vegan/vegetarian options. Huge portions — come hungry. Seasonal hours.

Local Myth Pizza

Artisan Pizza · Manson & Chelan

Award-winning hand-crafted artisan pizzas and calzones, fresh salads, housemade desserts, and 20 local NW beers and ciders on tap. A valley staple with locations in both Manson and Chelan.

Tapped Out Sports Bar

Bar & Grill · Manson

A fresh, energetic gathering space in downtown Manson with great drinks, live sports coverage, and a friendly atmosphere. Great for game nights or casual evenings with the group.

Lakeview Drive-In

Classic Drive-In · Manson Hwy

Famous fries by the bucket with housemade seasoning salt, fresh burgers, homemade tartar sauce, and hand-dipped milkshakes. A fun, nostalgic stop near Don Morse Park. Seasonal — check hours.

In Chelan (~15 min away)

Layla's Bar + Kitchen

Upscale Casual · Downtown Chelan

A local favorite for late lunch or dinner. Shareable tapas, classic cocktails, unique menu, and full bar. Consistently praised for friendly staff and a lively atmosphere.

The Albatross

American · Chelan

Highly rated with rave reviews for exceptional food and service. A well-rounded dining experience for a special night out — reserve ahead during summer.

Sorrento's Ristorante

Italian · South Shore

Named one of the Top 100 Scenic View Restaurants in America by OpenTable. Italian cuisine on the south shore of Lake Chelan — the views alone are worth the trip.

Apple Cup Café

Breakfast & Lunch · Chelan

A classic Chelan breakfast spot loved by locals and visitors alike. Hearty portions, friendly service, and a great place to fuel up before a day on the water.

Wine Country

Wineries &Tasting Rooms

With over 30 wineries in the Lake Chelan AVA, you could spend a whole vacation just sipping. The North Shore near Manson is especially rich — many are within a short drive or even walkable from downtown Manson.

North Shore & Manson Favorites

Benson Vineyards

North Shore Hillside · Estate Grown

Perched atop the hills with sweeping panoramic views of Lake Chelan. 100% estate-grown in a stunning Mediterranean-inspired setting. Known for bold Cabernet Sauvignon and Merlot. One of the most beautiful tasting experiences in the valley.

Cairdeas Winery

Rhône-Style · Downtown Manson

The Gaelic word for "friendship" — and the vibe here delivers. Rhône-style wines (Syrah, Grenache, Viognier) crafted in small batches with lake views and warm hospitality. A top pick among serious wine lovers.

Hard Row to Hoe

North Shore · Ask About the Name!

One of Washington's Top 22 Tasting Rooms. The story behind the name is one of Chelan's most entertaining tales — ask the staff! Estate vineyard wines with stunning lake views and a fun, welcoming atmosphere.

Radiance Winery

North Shore · Family Owned

Award-winning wines crafted with traditional techniques in a beautifully restored 100-year-old barn and scenic patio. Overlooks Lake Chelan and benefits from over 300 sunny days a year. A peaceful, gorgeous tasting experience.

Chelan Ridge Winery

North Shore · Gravity-Flow Estate

Family-owned gravity-flow winery producing classically-styled, handcrafted wines in small batches. Estate premium grapes, beautiful North Shore setting. They also have a Woodinville tasting room for the off-season.

Succession Wines

North Shore · Inspiring Story

Founded after a wildfire prompted owners Brock and Erica Lindsay to pursue their dream. Regularly sells out of each vintage. Great patio, impressive wines, and one of the most loyal followings in the valley.

Diffraction Wine

Downtown Manson · Family Friendly

Made by the Cairdeas team — approachable, everyday wines perfect for boat days and pizza nights. Retro arcade games, ice cream, and a laid-back family-friendly atmosphere. Grab a bottle to go!

VIBE Cellars

Downtown Manson · Italian-Inspired

Award-winning winery with roots in Northern Italy. Specializes in Italian-style varietals and French Bordeaux-style reds. Tasting room right on Manson's main street with great lake views from the patio.

🍷

Pro tip: Wapato Point Cellars and Wine Maker's Grill is located right at the other end of the resort — an easy stroll for a tasting or dinner without getting in the car. The Manson Chamber also coordinates limo wine tours if you want to make a day of it!

Did You Know?

AboutLake Chelan

50.5 miles of lake surrounded by vineyards, mountains, and small-town charm — with a few surprises along the way.

1,486ft

Lake Chelan's maximum depth, making it one of the deepest lakes in the world and the deepest in Washington State.

4th

Lake Chelan ranks as the 4th cleanest lake in the United States, behind only Lake Tahoe, Crater Lake, and Lake Coeur d'Alene.

300+

Days of sunshine per year in Chelan — more than Miami! That's why the fruit orchards and wine grapes thrive here.

30+

Wineries in the Lake Chelan AVA. The young volcanic soils and four distinct seasons produce award-winning vintages year after year.

25k

Population swells from ~4,000 residents to 25,000 people during peak summer months. You've picked a great place to be!

1914

The Chelan Ruby Theater opened — one of the oldest continuously running movie theaters in the entire Pacific Northwest.

🐊

Meet Ogopogo

Local legend says a lake monster named Ogopogo prowls the depths of Lake Chelan — with the body and legs of an alligator and the head and eyes of a serpent. Keep your eyes peeled on those early morning paddles...

🏔️

The Name "Chelan"

Chelan is a Salish Indian word ("Tsi-Laan") meaning "Deep Water" — fitting for a lake with nearly 1,500 feet of depth. The town's sister city is Tojo, Hyōgo, Japan.
